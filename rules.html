Rules

For Confessions

  • Don’t post anything that violates any relevant laws or the Facebook Community Standards.
  • Remember that this is a forum for anonymous confessions. Please don't ask general school questions if there's no reason you would need/want to be anonymous. Google, of course, is a good resource (especially if you use the site: operator to restrict your search to ucsc.edu), as are the “Official” Group of UCSC Students and Groups at UCSC.
  • No URLs.
  • Don’t submit posts that consist entirely of verbatim quotes from other sources.
  • No commerce of any kind.
  • It’s fine to put your email address in a confession, but only if it’s really yours. No other forms of contact information are allowed.
  • Don’t advertise parties.
  • Posts that are spammy, unintelligble, typed in ALL CAPS, or devoid of content will not be posted.
  • Don’t discuss shitting in someone’s mouth or any related acts. Before you ask, yes, this has been an issue and that’s why there’s a rule about it.

For Comments

  • Don’t be abusive towards other commenters.
  • Don’t post spam.

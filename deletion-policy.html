Deletion Policy

For Confessions

If you have a problem with a post, please send a Facebook message to the page or email administrator@ucsantacruzconfessions.com rather than reporting the post to Facebook so we can try to resolve the situation. Be sure to include a link to the offending post. I generally will only delete posts in one of two situations:

  • The post is in violation of the law, the Facebook Community Standards, or my rules. I often skim the submissions before posting, and offensive material sometimes slips through the cracks.
  • If you want a post that mentions your name deleted, I’ll delete it irregardless of its other content.

For Comments

Since Facebook doesn’t hold me responsible for comments, I generally recommend simply reporting offensive comments to Facebook. However, feel free to message the page or email administrator@ucsantacruzconfessions.com in cases of spam or a commenter repeatedly and consistently abusing other commenters in multiple instances. Please include links to relevant posts.

Bans are taken very seriously and are rarely applied. Lenience increases down this list:

  1. Profiles clearly created solely for posting spam comments are killed on sight.
  2. Profiles or pages with a fake identity are given the benefit of the doubt.
  3. Profiles with real identities but that don’t belong to UCSC affiliates will be banned if they’re being severly disruptive.
  4. Profiles with real identities that belong to UCSC students are rarely, if ever, banned.

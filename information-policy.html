Information Policy

This server, like all other web servers, logs the IP addresses of all computers that interact with it. This information is recorded for purely diagnostic and security purposes.

For the sake of protecting the anonymity of confessors, no attempt is made to associate IP addresses with individual confessions.

Aside from the publication of confessions on the page, no information will be released to law enforcement or any other party unless a valid subpoena is served.

Email Relay

To allow others to email you while still remaining anonymous yourself, enter your email address into the “Contact email” box when writing a confession. When posted, your confession will include an @ucsantacruzconfessions.com address. Any emails sent to this address will be relayed to you.

If you respond to any of these emails, you will no longer be anonymous and whoever you responded to will now see your real email address. The relay service is provided only so you can selectively decide who to make first contact with; it is not an anonymous chat service. On that same token, anyone sending emails to @ucsantacruzconfessions.com addresses will not be anonymous either.

Your relay will expire after 14 days. This is for two reasons:

  1. To prevent the page from becoming a harvesting ground for spammers
  2. To protect your anonymity; your email address will not be kept on record after your relay expires.

If you wish to have your relay terminated sooner than 14 days, please email administrator@ucsantacruzconfessions.com.

Relayed emails will be stripped down to plain text, so don't bother using fancy formatting or sending attachments.
